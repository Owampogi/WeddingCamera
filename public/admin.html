Setup Instructions:
  1. Make sure your server is running and accessible on your network
  2. Find your computer's local IP address (shown in terminal)
  3. Print this QR code or display it on screens at the venue
  4. Guests scan with their phone camera to open the camera app
  5. Each guest gets 50 shots (configurable in Settings)
  6. Photos appear in real-time on the Gallery page
